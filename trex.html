Intresting Facts about T-rex/Tyrannosaurus

  1. Tyrannosaurus rex are one of the largest dinosaurs to have walked the earth, lived 70 million years ago in what is now North America.

  2. Weighing in at 7.5 tons, standing 15-20 feet tall and 40 feet long

  3. T. rex was a fierce predator!

  4. The Tyrannosaurus had a life span of around 30 years

  5. Tyrannosaurus is from the Greek word meaning Tyrant Lizard

  6. Its arms were too short to reach its mouth.

  7. It lived in North America in river valleys and forests
