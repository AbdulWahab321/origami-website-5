Intresting Facts about Dragonflies

  1. Dragonflies don't sting and generally don't bite people.

  2. They have been around for 300 million years.

  3. When first hatched, the larva or nymphs live in the water for around a year.

  4. People in Indonesia like to eat dragonflies for a snack.

  5. Having a dragonfly land on your head is considered good luck.

  6. Some of the larger dragonflies live 6 or 7 years!

  7. Most of this time is spent in the larval form, beneath the water surface, catching other invertebrates

  8. The small damselflies live for a couple of weeks as free-flying adults.
