Intresting Facts about Triceratops

  1. Triceratops means three-horned-face in Greek.

  2. The triceratops' head is one of the largest of any land animal discovered.

  3. Some triceratops may have had as many as 800 teeth!

  4. They are a member of the ceratopsia suborder of dinosaurs.

  5. It probably wasn't a very fast dinosaur.

  6. There were actually reptiles on Earth before dinosaurs.

  7. About about 50 Triceratops skulls and some partial skeletons have been found. Triceratops was a late Ornithischian dinosaur, the order of bird-hipped, herbivorous dinosaurs
